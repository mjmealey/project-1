Chicken Spaghetti

This recipe serves to add a protein kick to an already delicious meal served by the gods of taste buds. Chicken is an ingenious add on to spaghetti which is already seemingly complete.

To add on to the wonderful recipe, this recipe is quick and easy to make which makes this meal wonderful on those days where you must rush out the door!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package of angel hair pasta
  • 2 cups of chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) of can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 3/4 (8 ounce) package of processed, cubed cheese
  • 1/2 (4 ounce) jar of sliced mushrooms, drained
  • Salt and pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes
  2. Add chopped chicken, condensed soup, diced tomatoes with chile peppers, processed cheese, mushrooms, salt, and pepper
  3. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through, about 5 minutes
