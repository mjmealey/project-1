Hummus Pizza

Description

Hummus pizza serves as a healthier alternative to the traditional pizza. Covered in hummus, vegetables and your cheese of choice, this delicious dish will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Hummus Pizza also takes little time to prep, clocking in at a 30 minute cook time, this makes for a fantastic meal option for convenience!

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
  • 1 cup of hummus spread
  • 1 1/2 cups of sliced bell peppers of any color
  • 1 cup of your favorite vegetables
  • 2 cups of cheese of your choosing

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees celcius
  2. Roll out pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  3. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust
  4. Arrange sliced peppers and (vegetable of choice) over the hummus, and top with (cheese of your choosing)
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes.

Slice and Serve!
